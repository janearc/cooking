# birthday cake (red velvet)

## Ingredients
- 2.5 cups (320g) all-purpose flour
- 1.5 cups (300g) granulated sugar
- 1 tsp cocoa powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
- 1 cup (240ml) vegetable oil
- 4 large eggs
- 1 cup (240ml) buttermilk
- 2 tsp white vinegar
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 2–3 tbsp red food coloring

### Cream Cheese Frosting
- 8 oz (225g) cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
- 4 cups (480g) powdered sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla extract

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch (23cm) round cake pans.  
2. **Mix dry ingredients:** In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.  
3. **Combine wet ingredients:** In a large bowl, beat butter, oil, eggs, buttermilk, vinegar, vanilla, and red food coloring until smooth.  
4. **Combine wet and dry:** Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet, mixing until just combined.  
5. **Bake:** Divide batter evenly between pans. Bake 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Let cool in pans 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.  
6. **Make frosting:** Beat cream cheese and butter until smooth. Gradually add powdered sugar and vanilla, beating until fluffy.  
7. **Assemble cake:** Spread frosting between layers, then cover top and sides. Decorate as desired.  
8. **Chill:** Let cake chill for at least 30 minutes before serving for best slicing.
