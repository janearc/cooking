# helpman sourdough discard biscuits

## Ingredients
- 188 g sourdough discard (50/50 flour/water)  
- 250 g all-purpose flour  
- 2 tsp baking powder  
- 1 tsp salt  
- 1 tbsp lindenhonig  
- 60–80 g cold butter, cut into small chunks  
- 120 ml milk (adjust as needed for dough consistency)  
- 2–3 tbsp fresh dill, finely chopped  

## Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 220 °C (425 °F).  
2. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt.  
3. Cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  
4. Add the sourdough discard, milk, and lindenhonig, and gently mix until just combined.  
   - Dough should be soft but not sticky.  
5. Fold in the chopped dill gently.  
6. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and gently pat to ~2 cm thickness.  
7. Cut into biscuits using a cutter or knife.  
8. Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment and bake for 12–15 minutes,  
   until golden brown.  
9. Cool slightly and enjoy warm.
