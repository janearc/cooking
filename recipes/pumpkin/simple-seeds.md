simple pumpkin seeds
---
prepare **pumpkin seeds** as defined elsewhere in this repository.

- 1 cup pumpkin seeds
- 3T coconut oil
- 3T coarse salt flakes
- 2T red pepper flakes
- black pepper to taste

add the pumpkin seeds to a bowl, coat thoroughly with coconut oil, add salt flakes and remaining ingredients. place on a cookie sheet and cook at 325Â°F for 15-20 minutes until crispy.
